## 1. Who We Are

**Operator:** Ruddia（ラディア）  
**Legal Entity:** 渡辺洋輝（個人事業主） / HIROKI WATANABE (Sole Proprietor)

Ruddia provides an AI personality runtime and companion platform ("the Service") that enables users to form long-term relationships with AI partners. The Service provides a personality layer — including memory, personality, and relationship management — accessible directly through our web application or via third-party integrations through our Being API. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2-1. Account Information
- Email address (Supabase Auth)
- Display name
- Age confirmation (18+ verification)

### 2-2. Conversation Data
- **Session logs:** Raw conversation messages between you and your AI partner. Retained for up to 30 days, then automatically deleted.
- **Memory data (persistent):** Structured information your AI partner remembers about you — your preferences, life context, relationship history, emotional patterns, and other details derived from conversations. Retained indefinitely while your account is active.
- **Patrol notes:** Periodic reflections and summaries generated by your AI partner based on accumulated memory data. These AI-generated notes help maintain personality continuity. Retained indefinitely while your account is active.

### 2-3. Sensitive Information

During conversations, you may voluntarily share sensitive information such as:
- Health conditions or mental health status
- Sexual orientation or gender identity
- Religious or philosophical beliefs
- Emotional or psychological states

We process this information **only** to provide and personalize the Service. We obtain your **explicit consent** for processing sensitive information through a dedicated consent mechanism during registration (see Section 10).

### 2-4. Usage Data
- Access logs (IP address, device information, connection timestamps)
- Feature usage metrics, error logs
- Session metadata (duration, frequency)

### 2-5. API Keys (BYOK)
- If you use the BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) feature, your third-party AI provider API keys are stored **encrypted at rest** using AES-256-GCM. Keys are decrypted only at the time of API calls and are never stored in plaintext or transmitted to any party other than the AI provider you selected.

### 2-6. Payment Information
- Payment processing is handled by Stripe. **We do not store your credit card information.** Stripe processes payments in accordance with PCI DSS standards.

## 4. How Your Data Is Processed by AI Providers

### 4-1. AI Processing Partners

To generate your AI partner's responses, we send your conversation input to third-party AI processing providers ("AI Providers"). Through our BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) system, you choose which AI provider to use and provide your own API key. Currently supported providers:

| Provider | Location | Purpose | Data Retention |
|---|---|---|---|
| Anthropic, PBC | United States | AI response generation | Deleted within 30 days per Anthropic's policy |
| OpenAI, Inc. | United States | AI response generation | Deleted within 30 days per OpenAI's API data usage policy |
| Google LLC | United States | AI response generation | Per Google's API terms of service |

**Your data is sent only to the provider you select.** We do not send your data to providers whose API keys you have not configured. We may add support for additional AI Providers in the future. This policy will be updated accordingly, and you will be notified of material changes.

### 4-2. Safeguards

- AI Providers process your data **solely under our instructions** and do not use it for their own purposes (including model training).
- We maintain Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with all AI Providers that include Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for international data transfers.
- Anthropic holds SOC 2 Type II certification.

### 4-3. API Clients (Being API)

When third-party developers integrate Ruddia's personality runtime via our Being API:

- **The API client is the data controller** for their end users' data. Ruddia acts as a **data processor**.
- API clients are responsible for obtaining appropriate consents from their end users, including disclosure of Ruddia's role as a data processor.
- Our Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with API clients specifies that Ruddia may use **anonymized and aggregated** data from API usage to research and improve the personality runtime. This is disclosed to API clients prior to contract execution.
- No individually identifiable data from API clients is used for research purposes without the API client's contractual agreement.
- API clients must inform their end users about Ruddia's data processing role and research use in their own privacy policies.

### 4-4. Authentication Provider

Email authentication is processed by Supabase. Your email address is transmitted to Supabase solely for authentication purposes (email verification and password reset). We maintain appropriate data processing agreements with Supabase.

## 7. Your Rights

Regardless of your location, you have the following rights:

### 7-1. Access
You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

### 7-2. Correction
You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.

### 7-3. Deletion
You can delete your data through the following methods:
- **Full memory deletion:** Delete all AI partner memories via in-app settings.
- **Category-based deletion:** Delete specific memory categories (clusters) via in-app settings.
- **Individual memory deletion:** Request deletion of specific memories by contacting us.
- **Account deletion:** Delete your entire account and all associated data via in-app settings.

Upon account deletion, all your data (account information, memory data, session logs, patrol notes) will be deleted within 30 days. Backup copies will be purged within 90 days. We will instruct our AI Providers to delete any data they retain.

### 7-4. Data Export (Portability)
You can download your memory data in a machine-readable format (JSON) via in-app settings.

### 7-5. Withdraw Consent
Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw consent at any time. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing performed before withdrawal.

### 7-6. How to Exercise Your Rights
- **Self-service:** Most rights can be exercised through in-app settings.
- **Contact us:** privacy@ruddia.com
- **Response time:** We will respond within 30 days (extendable by an additional 60 days for complex requests, with notice).

## 10. Consent Mechanisms

During registration, we collect the following consents:

1. **General consent:** Agreement to this Privacy Policy and Terms of Service (required to use the Service).
2. **Sensitive data consent:** Explicit, separate consent for the processing of sensitive information (health, sexual orientation, mental state, etc.) that you may share during conversations. This consent is collected via a **dedicated checkbox**, separate from the general consent.
3. **Research consent:** Consent for the use of your anonymized and aggregated data to research and improve Ruddia's memory system technology. This consent is **optional** and collected via a dedicated checkbox. You can use the Service without consenting to research use.
4. **Cross-border transfer consent:** Consent for the transfer of your data to AI Providers located outside your country of residence (see Section 5).

All consents can be withdrawn at any time through in-app settings. Withdrawing general consent requires account deletion. Withdrawing sensitive data consent will result in deletion of sensitive information from your AI partner's memory. Withdrawing research consent will exclude your data from future research activities (previously anonymized and aggregated data that can no longer be linked to you may remain in research datasets).
